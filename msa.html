Brand Identity • Website design

The Muslim Students’ association is University of Carleton’s largest student club. For this project, I worked closely with the club’s president and project lead as I was in charge of rebranding the club’s identity. The rebrand included creating a new site, launching a merchandise store, designing membership cards and several other props.

Conceptualizing the logo revolved around meeting three client requirements: to represent a welcoming atmosphere, to include an element of Islamic art as well as an element unique of Carleton University.
