Trend Micro Icon Set

Icon Design • Illustration

Designed at McMillan
Art Direction: Michael Zavacky
Account Management: Phil Radok
Design: Michael Zavacky, Iman Ghader

Trend Micro has a large icon library that it’s employees use in presentations, on documents and various other things. Their icon set needed to be up to date with their current brand and refined to be be simple and easily understandable.

Under the art direction and supervision of Michael Zavacky at McMillan, I was tasked to help complete refinening this Trend Micro icon library. We worked together on refining the concepts of each icon and redesigning it to have minimum complexity and maintain legibility on small scale. Zavacky had developed set guidelines for the icons to have a consistent look. I later extended the use of these guidelines to create more icons or refine old ones.
